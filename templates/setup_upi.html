Terms & Conditions

UPI ID Terms of Use

1. UPI ID Format:

  • Format: username@provider
  • Username can contain: letters, numbers, dots, underscores, hyphens, and @ symbols
  • Examples: john.doe@easycash, user123@easycash, ahmed@1@easycash, email_address@easycash
  • UPI ID cannot be changed once set

2. Privacy & Sharing:

  • Your UPI ID will be visible to other EasyCash users
  • Users can find you by your UPI ID
  • Your username will be displayed when someone searches your UPI ID

3. Security:

  • Never share your PIN with anyone
  • Report suspicious transactions immediately
  • EasyCash will never ask for your PIN via email or phone

4. Acceptable Use:

  • Use UPI ID only for legitimate transactions
  • Do not use offensive or inappropriate UPI IDs
  • Comply with all applicable laws and regulations
